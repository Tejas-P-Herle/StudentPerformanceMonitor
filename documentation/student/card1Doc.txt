A Student's performance in percentage of a given set of exams or subjects.
In the given set of exams or subjects, a student's performance based on percentage being displayed with the best percentage obtained by the class, the avarage percentage obtained by the class and the least percentage by the class.
In the given set of exams or subjects, a student's performance based on percentile being displayed with the best percentile obtained by the class(Always 100), the avarage percentile obtained by the class and the least percentile by the class.
A Student's performance based on percentage and percentile over the span of all the exams.
