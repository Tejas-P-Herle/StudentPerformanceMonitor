About SPM


A student performs better than he did last time and holds a better position in the class but his marks show otherwise. This may be because of many factors like the toughness of the paper, the toughness of the concepts for the exam etc. So to get to know the student's true performance and to bring to light the student's effort, SPM has been created. SPM - Student Performance Monitor is a Data Science project for educationists aimed at helping in student performance analysis through graphical charts. It helps in discovering a student's performance, class performance and others factors in unconventional ways which usually go unnoticed.

Advantages of using SPM


* Students position with respect to the class is clearly seen
* Students improvement with respect to the last exam is displayed
* Students strengths and weaknesses are displayed
* Subject-wise performance is seen
* Gives a clear idea of a students performance
