About SPM


A student performes better than he did last time and hold a better position in the class but his marks show otherwise. This may be because of many factors like the toughness of the paper, the thoughness of the consepts for the exam etc. So to get to know the student's true performance and to bring to light the student's effort, SPM has been created. SPM short for Student Performance Monitor is a Data Science project for educationists aimed at helping in student performance analysis in graphical foramts. It helps in discovering a student's performance, class performance and others factors in unconventional ways which usually go unnoticed.

Advantages of using SPM


* Students position with respect to the class is clearly seen
* Students improvement with respect to the last exam is displayed
* Students strengths and weaknesses are displayed
* Subject-wise performance is seen
* Gives a clear idea of a students performance

Class Performance

Indicates class performance rather than a single student's performance. Its graphs include, the percentage and percentile of every student of a given class in a given exam, the improvement of students in each subject or the improvement of a student over the period of all the exams.It even displays the top ten performers of a given class of all the exams or of a given exam.

Student Performance

Gives importance to a single student's performance rather than the whole class. Its graphs include, percentage of each subject or all the exams (for side-by-side comparision of each subject's performance or exams), a student's relative position based on percentage and percentile with respect to the class best, class least and the class average and the student's improvement based on both percentage and percentile over the period of all the exams. The student's performance of each subject over the period of all the exams is displayed.
